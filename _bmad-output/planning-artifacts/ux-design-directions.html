Evaluation Criteria:

  • Direction 1 (Bold & Energetic): Strong celebration-focused design with gradient accents and prominent lion mascot. Best for users who want motivation and visual energy.
  • Direction 2 (Clean & Minimal): Maximum simplicity with circular progress and list-based macros. Best for users who want distraction-free data at a glance.
  • Direction 3 (Card-Heavy Simple): Balanced approach combining Simple's card style with Duolingo's friendly nav. Best for general appeal and familiar patterns.
  • Direction 4 (Dense & Efficient): More information per screen with compact spacing. Best for power users who want quick overview of all metrics.
