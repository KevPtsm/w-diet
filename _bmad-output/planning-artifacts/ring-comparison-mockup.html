Comparison Analysis:

Version A: Segmented Ring

  • PRO: Shows macro distribution visually
  • PRO: Educational (calories come from macros)
  • PRO: More data in same space
  • CON: Higher visual complexity
  • CON: Requires learning to interpret
  • CON: Slightly harder to scan total calories

Version B: Simple Ring

  • PRO: Crystal clear calorie progress
  • PRO: Familiar pattern (Apple Health rings)
  • PRO: Lower cognitive load
  • PRO: Faster to scan at a glance
  • CON: Doesn't show macro distribution
  • CON: Less educational/innovative

Recommendation: A/B test both versions in POC. Track user comprehension, engagement, and performance.
